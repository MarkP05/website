About Me

My name is Mark Pfister, and I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a 3+1 concentration in Cybersecurity at Quinnipiac University. I chose this field because I've been passionate about technology and programming from a young age. One of my long-term goals is to design and develop my own video games. My academic background includes advanced proficiency in Java, a working knowledge of C#, and a solid foundation in networking principles and the broader concepts of Computer Science.

Outside of academics, I enjoy spending my free time playing video games, which has been a long-standing hobby of mine. During the winter months, I often travel north with my family to go snowboarding—something I’ve been doing since early childhood and continue to enjoy today. At present, my time is mostly dedicated to school-related work and gaming, both of which play a major role in my personal and professional growth.
